Irish Stew

Ingredients

  • Diced beef
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Spuds
  • 2 cups of beef stock
  • Diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Instructions

You'll need 2 saucepans; one medium pan to boil the potatoes slightly and one large one to combine and stew all ingredients

  1. Boil potatoes in it's own saucepan
  2. Slice carrots and onions to your liking
  3. Brown the onions and garlic in a large saucepan
  4. Add the beef and brown evenly
  5. Add the stock and diced carrots and the boiled spuds
  6. Season with salt, pepper and and turmeric or other herbs you like
  7. Simmer at low heat for 45 mins
